Looking at Submerged on Steam, I thought, This is amazing! I wasn't far wrong.

You are Miku, on a quest to find supplies to treat your injured brother. Strange, once-human creatures watch from afar, coming closer each time you bring something back to base.

Gameplay

Boating and climbing are Submerged's main activities, neither of which is very difficult. Boating has an odd lack of momentum- you stop immediately on releasing the keys. Was this intentional, or just the product of lazy developers?

The game's store page boasts "increasingly difficult navigation puzzles". Translation: some buildings are bigger and have more possible routes to the item. There are no environmental factors to impede your progress- not so much as a fatigue mechanic.

Stories

There's no dialog in Sumberged. Instead the game's plot is doled out in simple pictographs. Pretentious? Perhaps. But it's fairly obvious that a disaster drowned the city.
